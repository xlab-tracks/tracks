## Running the model

You now have the whole regime model, and everything the analysis takes is already in your hands from the previous lesson and readings: read the three states, price each technique's basket against the regime's price table, and ask which channel — usefulness, evidence, direct — its payoff flows through. What the prices and channels say, the portfolio should reflect.

Every portfolio answer in this module takes a three-part shape:

1. **The allocation** — how the effort splits across the technique menu.
2. **Where the marginal slice of effort goes, and through which channel.**
3. **Which spend binds** — the aisle the team would trade any other for.
